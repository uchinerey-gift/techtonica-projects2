Instructions

  1. Soak the chiles in hot water for 15 min until soft.
  2. Blend chiles with onion, garlic, vinegar + spices for sauce.
  3. Marinate beef in sauce 1 hour (overnight best).
  4. Cook beef low and slow until tender.
  5. Dip tortillas in broth and fill with beef + cheese.
  6. Pan-fry until golden and crisp.
