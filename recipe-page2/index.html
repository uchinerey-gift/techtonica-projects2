Birria Tacos

Authentic Birria tacos are tender, flavorful Mexican tacos made with slow-cooked beef, rich spices, and melted cheese, served with a side of savory consomm√© for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into chunks
  • 4 dried guajillo chiles
  • 2 dried ancho chiles
  • 1 white onion, quartered
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas and shredded cheese for serving
